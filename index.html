INFLUENCERS MANAGEMENT

Influencer marketing is bridging the right people to increase brand awareness and high impact conversations with customers and turn them into conversions. Let our magic touch to develop your influencer marketing strategies catered to your brand to have a successful influencer campaign.

Micro-influencers - Anyone who is active on social media with less than 10,000 followers. They normally have higher engagement rates than macro influencers, as they share quality reviews of the products they love, not because they are paid to.

Alpha & Macro Influencers - Well known in the market with a minimum follower count between 10,000 - 1 million. These influencers have a decent engagement rate with good reach. They could also help you create a CTA to your website as they have the swipe up function.

Mega Influencers - The most expensive but the most well known influencers, they could be actors, musicians, social media icons. Influencers with over 1 million followers would cost a ton of money and they are typically used for generating awareness for your brand as their engagement rate are typically 2-5%

Our Process For Influencers Management:
Dedicate influencer that is best suited for your brand - Our manager would understand your business and develop a campaign strategy for your brand

Influencer proposal - Campaign budget would be negotiated to get the right influencer for your brand. You are able to review and approve before anything goes live

Monitoring - We would monitor influencer’s daily activities and strategize based on performance

Reporting - Campaign performance report would be provided to you, based on the insights and key learnings we could further improve for the next campaign
